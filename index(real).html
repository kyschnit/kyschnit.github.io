About Me

I'm a rising sophomore studying computer science at the University of Michigan. I'm still discovering my passions within the field, but I have enjoyed my experiences with web design and Android app development. I'm willing to try anything at least once, and I'm constantly expanding my working knowledge. My primary programming experience lies in C++ and Java. I'm currently a software engineering intern for Epsilon Imaging, Inc.

I grew up surrounded by technology, so it seemed almost inevitable that I would major in computer science. It is something that has shaped my life, and I hope to use it to make a positive impact on the world around me.

Outside of programming, my interests include reading, running, biking, hiking, camping, playing video games, and 3D modeling with CAD software (to view some of my CAD work, click here). I am a member of MASA, the University of Michigan's student rocketry club. I also help to lead Ten for Men, a youth group for students of my high school.
