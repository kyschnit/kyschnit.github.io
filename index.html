About Me

I'm a rising junior studying computer science at the University of Michigan. I'm still discovering my passions within the field, but I have enjoyed my experiences with web development and Android app development. I'm pursuing a minor in entrepreneurship, and I'm very interested in the fast-paced, innovative culture of startups. I'm always willing to learn something new, and I'm constantly expanding my working knowledge. My primary programming experience lies in C++, Javascript, and Angular. I'm currently a Software Engineering Intern at MathWorks in the Engineering Development Group!

I grew up surrounded by technology, so it seemed almost inevitable that I would major in computer science. It is something that has shaped my life, and I hope to use it to make a positive impact on the world around me.

Outside of programming, my interests include traveling, reading, running, biking, hiking, camping, baking, playing video games, and 3D modeling with CAD software. I am a member of Michigan Hackers, a student computer science organization at the University of Michigan. I also help to lead Ten for Men, a youth group for students at the high school I attended. I stay active as a member of the University of Michigan Club Triathlon team.
